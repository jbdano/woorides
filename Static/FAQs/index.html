Frequently Asked Questions

General

How can I book a ride?

Either wave us down in the street or call us directly at (508) 966-8439 between the hours of 10pm and 2am Thursday through Saturday nights.

Do you only take cash? How do I give you my money?

We take cash, Venmo, and can even swipe a credit/debit card. Either way, don’t forget to tip your driver. That way they pedal extra fast ;)

Where can I find you for a ride?

We operate mainly in the Grid and Canal Districts, but we venture to Shrewsbury Street and occasionally elsewhere as well. Whether you’re enjoying a farm fresh dinner at Armsby Abbey, a brew at the Beer Garden, or a late night snack at Dive Bar, we’ve got you covered.

Peddling these things seems hard. How far will you take me?

You’re right. It is hard. But that doesn’t stop us. Our typical range for a ride is 1 mile but exceptions can be made if you smile a lot and tell us how awesome we are.

How many people can ride at once?

For safety sake (and for our drivers’ backs) and per manufacturer specifications we limit rides to 600 pounds or the number of people that can actually sit on the seat (typically this is two adults and one child).

Can I drive the pedicab?

As much as we would like to reverse roles and take a well-deserved break, we unfortunately can’t make this happen. Our insurance wouldn’t be happy with us.
